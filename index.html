How it works

1

Head to My predictions and enter your name and entry team name at the top — this identifies your submission.

2

Predict a score for every game from the groups through to the final.

3

Once you have entered all the scores click the Submit Your Form button — if it's greyed out it means you are missing a result or have not entered your team/name at the top.

4

The scoring works as follows:

+1One point, during Group stage matches, for each correct guess of the number of goals a team will score in that match (regardless of result) (max 144 pts)
+1One point, during Group stage matches, for each correct guess of the result (i.e. home win, draw, or away win) regardless of goals scored (max 72 pts)
+1One point, at the end of the group stage, for each correct guess of a team's placement in a group (1st, 2nd, 3rd or 4th) (max 48 pts)
+2Two points for each correctly guessed team progressing into the Round of 32 (max 64 pts)
+3Three points for each correct team advancing into the Round of 16, Quarter-finals and Semi-finals (max 90 pts)
+6Six points for correctly guessing the losing finalist (max 6 pts)
+12Twelve points for correctly guessing the winner (max 12 pts)
Overall tie-breaks are settled by the closest number of non-penalty-shootout goals across the tournament.
Group result example: You guess Team A 1–2 Team B. If the actual result is 1–2 you get 3 pts; 0–2 you get 2 pts (Team B goals & Team B winning); 2–2 you get 1 pt (Team B goals only); 1–0 you get 1 pt (Team A goals only); 0–0 you get 0 pts.
